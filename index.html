Some of Coltrane's most important Albums:

  • 1958 - Blue Train

    Coltrane's debut album was released on the Blue Note Record label. The band included Paul Chambers on bass, Philly Joe Jones on drums, Kenny Drew on piano, Lee Morgan on trumpet and Curtis Fuller on trombone.

  • 1959 - Kind of Blue (as a sideman with Miles Davis)
  • 1960 - Giant Steps
  • 1960 (10/21 - 10/26) - My Favorite Things; Coltrane Plays the Blues; Coltrane's Sound; Coltrane Jazz
  • 1962 - Ballads
  • 1964 - A Love Supreme
